In-App Touch Control

  • Pro Steering: Rotate your phone left and right to guide your vehicle smoothly across any racing title.
  • Analog Pedals: Slide your thumb downward over the Gas or Brake regions for progressive, high-accuracy telemetry.
  • Quick Shifters: Tap the left paddle to shift down, or the right paddle to shift up instantly.
  • Handbrake Click: Tap the dedicated button on the top-left to trigger an immediate handbrake lock for tight drifts.
  • Extra Buttons (×5): Five assignable buttons located around the steering wheel. You can customize them in-game for any function you need — such as headlights, wipers, turn signals, camera views, or engine start/stop, and more.
  • Custom Settings: Tap the wrench icon on the top-right corner to access configuration parameters and axis adjustments.

طريقة التحكم داخل التطبيق

  • توجيه احترافي: قم بإمالة الهاتف يميناً ويساراً للتحكم بمسار السيارة وتوجيهها بسلاسة تامة في أي لعبة.
  • دواسات تناسبية (Analog): اسحب إبهامك للأسفل فوق منطقة البنزين أو الفرامل الحصول على تسارع وتباطؤ تدريجي دقيق جداً.
  • ناقل حركة سريع: اضغط على المقبض الأيسر لخفض التروس، أو المقبض الأيمن لرفع التروس بشكل فوري.
  • كبسة الفرامل اليدوية: اضغط على الزر المخصص أعلى اليسار لتفعيل كبح الفرامل اليدوية فوراً والقيام بدريفتات محكمة.
  • أزرار إضافية (×5): خمسة أزرار قابلة للتخصيص موزعة حول عجلة القيادة. يمكنك تخصيصها في اللعبة لأي وظيفة تحتاجها — مثل الإضاءة، المساحات، إشارات الانعطاف، تغيير كاميرا السيارة، أو زر تشغيل/ايقاف السيارة، و المزيد.
  • قائمة الإعدادات: اضغط على أيقونة المفك أعلى اليمين لفتح قائمة الخيارات المتقدمة وتعديل حساسات المحاور.

Network Performance Optimizer

If your Wi-Fi network is slow or unstable and you want to get the absolute lowest latency, simply turn on your mobile's Portable Hotspot and connect your PC directly to that hotspot network. You don't even need an active internet data plan on your phone; as long as both devices reside on the same hotspot network, you will experience a lightning-fast, rock-solid response. However, if your home local network is already fast, you don't need this method and can connect normally over standard Wi-Fi.

Setup Guide

Step 1: Install vJoy Driver

Download and install vJoy. Open "Configure vJoy", ensure X, Y, and Z axes are checked, and click Apply.

Step 2: Install Mobile App Safely

Download the Android APK. If your device prompts a security warning because it is installed from an external source outside the Play Store, ignore it and choose 'Install anyway' to complete setup safely.

Step 3: Connect & Drive

Start the PC Client software. Ensure both devices are connected to the same local network for seamless zero-delay data transmission.

FAQ

Why does Android show a warning during installation +
This is completely normal. Android displays security notifications for any application or package installed directly from an external developer source outside of Google Play. The application is completely clean and safe.
Why does Windows display a SmartScreen warning when running the PC Client +
Windows Defender often triggers a warning flag for custom standalone server utilities because they are distributed externally rather than through the Microsoft Store registry. Your system is perfectly safe; all you need to do is click on "More Info" and then select "Run anyway" to initiate the connection.
Does this application work across all PC simulator and arcade racing games +
Yes! Because VELOX feeds telemetry directly into the industry-standard vJoy driver, your PC registers the input as a native hardware steering wheel configuration. This means it offers global compatibility across all virtual driving software, simulators, and open-world arcade titles.
